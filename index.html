About Me.

Remedi is a rising Neo-Soul and Hip-Hop artist hailing from Las Vegas, known for blending poetic lyricism with heart and soul. As one of the few Black female voices cutting through the noise in her city’s music scene, she brings raw authenticity, emotional depth, and a style that’s entirely her own. Her presence on stage is undeniable—whether performing at exclusive galas or iconic venues like Area15, Remedi commands attention with every word. In a genre often oversaturated with imitation, she stands out simply by being real.

Why me? The stage lights up when I do.
